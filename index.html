Ruby Annotations

EPUB Markdown supports HTML5 ruby .

Mono-ruby

What you write

{君|くん}{子|し}は{和|わ}して{同|どう}ぜず

What you get

<ruby>君<rt>くん</ruby><ruby>子<rt>し</ruby>は<ruby>和<rt>わ</ruby>して<ruby>同<rt>どう</ruby>ぜず

What you see

君くん子しは和わして同どうぜず

Mono-ruby for compound words

What you get

<ruby>鬼<rt>き</rt>門<rt>もん</rt></ruby>の<ruby>方<rt>ほう</rt>角<rt>がく</rt></ruby>を<ruby>凝<rt>ぎょう</rt>視<rt>し</rt></ruby>する

What you see

What you get

<p>平成<span class="tcy">20</span>年<span class="tcy">4</span>月<span class="tcy">16</span>日に</p>

What you see

平成20年4月16日に

Footnotes

What you write

That's some text with a footnote.[^1]

[^1]: And that's the footnote.

What you get

<div class="footnotes" epub:type="footnotes">
<hr />
<ol>

<li>
<div id="fn_1" class="footnote" epub:type="footnote">
<p>And that's the footnote.
   <a href="#fnref_1">&#9166;</a></p>
</div>
</li>

</ol>
</div>

What you write

That's some text with a footnote. 1

  1. And that's the footnote. ⏎

Page Numbers

Page number as content element

What you write

Lorem ipsum dolor sit amet, [%%23]consectetur adipisicing elit,

What you get

<p>Lorem ipsum dolor sit amet, <span id="pagenum_23" class="pagenum" title="23" epub:type="pagebreak">23</span>consectetur adipisicing elit, </p>

What you see

Lorem ipsum dolor sit amet, 23consectetur adipisicing elit,
